# Echo_AI
Echo AI is a research-oriented, modular artificial intelligence system designed to support structured clinical documentation, AI-assisted consultation workflows, and human-in-the-loop medical decision support.

Echo AI is not a diagnostic system.
It is designed to assist clinicians by organizing information, generating structured summaries, and improving workflow efficiency â€” with final authority always retained by licensed professionals.


No real patient data is stored in this repository

All example data is synthetic or fully de-identified

Audio recordings and raw sensitive inputs are never committed

This project follows privacy-by-design principles

Echo AI is a research and demonstration system only.
It is NOT intended for clinical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency use.
All AI-generated content must be reviewed and approved by licensed professionals.

Echo AI is designed to integrate with a client application (e.g. Flutter-based mobile app) through structured APIs and clearly defined payload schemas.

Human confirmation is required before final outputs

AI results are traceable and reviewable

Deletion policies can be enforced post-approval
